My DSA Journey

When I first started to learn DSA, it was quite difficult to understand. I struggled to grasp the concepts until I decided to draw each iteration on pen and paper. This hands-on approach helped me visualize the algorithms better and led me to create a visualization tool that enables users to easily visualize DSA concepts and gain a deeper understanding.
